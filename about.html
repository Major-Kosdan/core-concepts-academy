Who we are

Core Concepts Academy started with one clear idea: students don’t fail because they are weak, they fail when concepts aren’t taught in a way they can truly understand. It all began when Nnachi Kosisochukwu Daniel started tutoring 100-level students at the University of Nigeria, Nsukka, in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics. At first, lessons were one-on-one, at different times and places, with no formal venue.

By 300-level, we had a space where students could learn together. In final year, the team grew with additional tutors, helping more students and making learning structured, clear, and effective. We’ve helped hundreds of students turn fear into curiosity and confusion into understanding. Our lessons focus on the core concepts first, followed by guided practice to build mastery and confidence. We care about outcomes, not just grades, but real understanding.

Now, as graduates, we’ve taken Core Concepts online to reach students beyond our campus. Through live online sessions, we continue our mission, making tough topics simple, helping students overcome challenges, and giving them the tools to succeed academically and build confidence in their learning journey. From small beginnings to structured online programs, our focus remains the same: teach the core concepts, everything else follows.

Over the years, we’ve helped hundreds of first-year university students and UTME candidates regain their confidence, turning fear into curiosity and confusion into understanding. Our teaching is built on classroom experience and practical exam preparation. We break big ideas into manageable parts, show real-life applications, and guide practice that leads to measurable improvement. We focus on results: quicker understanding, steady progress, and lasting mastery of core concepts. From tutoring neighbours to teaching entire cohorts online, our mission has stayed the same: teach the core, and everything else follows.

“We don’t teach tricks. We teach the logic. Once the logic is clear, students begin to think for themselves.”
— N. K. Daniel, Founder

Our Mission

To help first-year university students and UTME candidates master challenging topics through clear explanations, guided practice, and mentorship. We aim to build strong foundations, confidence, and real understanding.

Our Vision

To be the leading academic partner across West Africa where students move from confusion to clarity, overcome challenges with ease, and achieve success with confidence.

Our Teaching Philosophy

Concept First

We start with the core idea. When students understand the 'why', the 'how' becomes easy.

Active Problem Solving

Students tackle step-by-step problems with guidance. Practice builds understanding and confidence.

Exam Alignment

We teach exam-ready skills: clear answers, time-saving tricks, and how to avoid common mistakes.

Feedback Loop

Focused feedback helps students improve fast and track progress every week.

Meet the founder

Nnachi Kosisochukwu Daniel

A visionary tutor and tech-minded educator, Nnachi believes that strong fundamentals and digital tools can transform learning across Africa.

Nnachi graduated in Computer Science from the University of Nigeria, Nsukka and began teaching because he saw how capable students struggled with presentation and structure — not intelligence. He builds lessons that prioritize reasoning, context and exam-readiness. Beyond tutoring, he builds digital tools to help students review faster, track progress, and practice with purpose.

Under his leadership the Academy focuses on outcomes: clear concept maps, measurable progress, and a culture of curiosity.
